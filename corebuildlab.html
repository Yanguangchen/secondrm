Why This Lab

Good IR isn’t just compliant disclosure—it’s a repeatable operating model that creates predictability, improves market understanding, and builds investor confidence over time. This Lab helps leadership teams move from ad-hoc IR to a disciplined quarterly rhythm, using your real numbers, your constraints, and your stakeholder reality.

Programme Details (4 sessions)

Four focused sessions × 2 hours across 4–6 weeks.

Each session ends with a clear, usable output and ownership for what comes next.

Session 1: IR North Star

Align leadership on why IR exists, what problems it must solve, and what success looks like.

Output: IR Mandate & Objectives Note (co-written)

Session 2: IR engine

Calendar, targeting & monthly check-in

Build the operating rhythm: cadence, stakeholder priorities, and management check-in.

Outputs: 12-month IR calendar, targeting list, Monthly IR Check-in template

Session 3: Equity Story That Travels

Business & financial blocks + peer context

Translate operations + numbers into a Street-friendly narrative anchored in peer context.

Outputs: Equity story backbone + peer comparison grid

Session 4: Disclosure Discipline & Rehearsal

Reporting, disclosure & rehearsal; IR Charter

Apply a disclosure checklist to a real announcement/results release, run a mock investor meeting/mini earnings call, and lock in governance.

Outputs: disclosure checklist applied, rehearsal + Q&A bank, IR Charter (co-drafted)
