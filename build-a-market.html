Programme Details

How It Works

Four focused sessions × 2 hours across 4–6 weeks. Each session ends with a clear, usable output and ownership for what comes next.

Session 1: IR mandate & capital‑market problem statement

Align senior management and IRO on why IR exists, what problems it must solve, and what success looks like; co‑write an IR Mandate & Objectives Note (with a short Board drop‑in recommended where available).

Session 2: IR engine — calendar, targeting & monthly check‑in

Build a 12‑month IR calendar, a target stakeholder list, and a one‑page monthly IR check‑in template.

Session 3: Business & financial blocks + peer story

Translate operations and numbers into a Street‑friendly narrative and peer context; draft the equity story backbone and peer comparison grid.

Session 4: Reporting, disclosure & rehearsal; IR Charter

Apply a reporting & disclosure checklist to a real announcement/results release, run a mock investor meeting/mini earnings call using the equity story + Q&A bank, and co‑draft your IR Charter.

Delivery

Delivered live — your office (Singapore) or live online. In‑office delivery is recommended for faster alignment and real‑time decisions.

Availability

Registrations are reviewed on a rolling basis and intake is limited each month to ensure quality and fit.

Who This Is For

Leadership teams ready to commit time and decisions — typically CEO + CFO + IR/Finance lead (up to 4 participants). This is for teams who want an operating system and outputs they can use immediately.
